Akira Toriyama

The Life and Works of Akira Toriyama

  • 1955 - Born in Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture, Japan
  • 1978 - Begins his career as a manga artist
  • 1980 - Creates his first major success, Dr. Slump
  • 1984 - Launches Dragon Ball, which becomes a global phenomenon
  • 1995 - Dragon Ball manga series concludes
  • 1999 - Designs characters for the video game Chrono Trigger
  • 2006 - Involved in the creation of Blue Dragon for Xbox 360
  • 2013 - Writes Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods movie
  • 2015 - Dragon Ball Super anime series begins
  • 2022 - Continues to influence the Dragon Ball franchise

Akira Toriyama's work has inspired generations of artists and entertained millions worldwide. His unique art style and storytelling have made him one of the most influential manga artists in history.
